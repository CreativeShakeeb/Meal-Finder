Meal Finder App

Welcome to the Meal Finder App! This platform allows you to search for delicious recipes based on various ingredients and dietary preferences. With a clean and user-friendly interface, finding the perfect meal has never been easier.

Functionality: The website offers a variety of features including:

  • Recipe search by ingredients, meal name and dietary preferences
  • Detailed recipe information including ingredients and nutritional values
  • Pagination for easy navigation through search results
  • Responsive design for optimal viewing on any device

Instructions: Follow the Instructions for better functionality:

  • Preferably search for a single word, such as a category of food (e.g., beef) or an ingredient (e.g., tomato).
  • Reload the page if the random meals are not shown or unavaiable on pagination (only when no search is performed).
  • Make sure your internet connection is fast; otherwise, the meals won't be shown.
  • Please contact me with any inquiries or if you encounter any issues.

API: This website fetches data from the Edamam Recipe Search API.

Developer: Developed by Shakeeb. You can contact Shakeeb at shakeeb182@gmail.com.

Note: The API has a search limit of 20,000 requests per month. Once this limit is reached, searches will be disabled until the count resets at the beginning of the next month.
